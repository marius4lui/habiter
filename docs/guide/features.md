# Key Features

Habiter isn't just a list; it's a designed experience.

## The "Glow" Design
The interface features a unique **Glassmorphism** style with animated background orbs.
- **Dynamic Background**: The "Glow Orbs" shift and move, creating a living interface.
- **Glass Elements**: Navigation bars and cards feature a frosted glass effect for a modern feel.

## Habit Tracking
Track your daily routines with ease.
- **Frequency**: Set habits to repeat Daily, Weekly, or on custom days.
- **Streaks**: Keep your streak alive to build momentum.
- **Analytics**: View detailed charts of your progress in the Analytics tab.

## Dark Mode
We prioritize your eye health. The app defaults to a system-aware theme but shines best in our custom **Cozy Dark Mode**, perfect for night-time reviews of your day.
